What is ambapay?

ambapay is a digital wallet system for users to securely manage and move money. It supports transfers using cards as well as Apple Pay and Google Pay.

Send and request money

  • Send money to contacts or newly added recipients
  • Request money from contacts directly within the app
  • Receive notifications for requests and completed donations

Security first

Most pages are protected with biometrics for strong, user-friendly authentication.

Do more with ambapay

  • Purchase tickets to events during event season
  • Manage your cards and recipients with simple, clear controls
